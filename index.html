A Peer to Peer Energy Trading System

To accelerate the transition to sustainable energy on a global scale, there should be rewards to those who make the transition to sustainable energy. A system that allows those who make the transition to sustainable energy to be rewarded. If you buy a Tesla vehicle, you will be rewarded in a p2p energy trading system.

Miners that use fossil fuels can purchase emissions(or carbon credits) in bitcoin from groups and individuals participating in a p2p energy trading system.

Through a p2p energy system, Chinese miners that use fossil fuels can offset their carbon footprints by purchasing credits in bitcoin from Miners in El Salvador using renewable energy.

A p2p energy trading system will encourage individuals and groups to switch to sustainable energy voluntarily, to ultimately help solve environmental problems caused by energy consumption and make positive contributions to climate change.

Creating an Entire Sustainable Energy Ecosystem

A p2p energy trading system has a reward system to encourage participation in the transition initiatives to sustainable energy the transition initiatives to sustainable energy.This reward system will bolster further growth and expansion of the sustainable energy ecosystem.

Emissoin trading & Sustainable Development Mechanism

Climate change is not a local issue but a global problem so we can address it with carbon emission trading with carbon emission trading.

Each of the groups and individuals involved in mining in the crypto ecosystem will be allocated carbon emission allowances. Miners have to most likely trade emission allowances with other Miners depending on the type of energy they use for mining.

Miners that use renewable energy, for example, will have emission allowances to spare as their greenhouse gas emissions are reduced and will make profits by selling these surplus credits to the p2p energy trading system. On the other hand, miners that use fossil fuel energy will have their greenhouse gas emissions exceed the quota and will need to purchase emission allowances, thereby being responsible for their emissions and contributing to the sustainable growth ecosystem.

As a result, a peer-to-peer energy trading system is the most efficient solution to sustainable energy transition in the crypto ecosystem. And p2p energy trading system allows the systematical implementation of a sustainable crypto ecosystem.
